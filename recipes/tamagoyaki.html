Tamagoyaki

Description

Sweet yet savory, Tamagoyaki (Japanese rolled omelette), makes a delightful Japanese breakfast or side dish for your bento lunches.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • Nuetral-flavored Oil
  • Dashi
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Whisk eggs
  3. Combine seasonings
  4. Combine eggs and seasonings
  5. Heat pan and pur mixture in a thin layer
  6. Roll the omlette gently
